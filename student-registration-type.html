Important Information

  • New Students: Use this option for students who are joining your school for the first time. The system will automatically generate a unique admission number.
  • Existing Students: Use this option for students who were already enrolled before implementing this system. You'll need to enter their existing admission number manually.
  • Data Validation: All student information will be validated to ensure accuracy and prevent duplicates.
  • Guardian Information: Complete guardian details are required for both registration types.
